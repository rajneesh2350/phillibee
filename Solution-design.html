Canteen Management

A school is not just about classes. It is much more than classes in the school. For students, to study well, requires a good food and having good largely depends on a swift and convenient canteen process at school.

Secureye canteen management application assists administrators from placing an order to selecting food to making a payment. Generally the entire canteen process is manual and is susceptible to human errors in accounting. The automated process eliminates human errors completely, and this improvement reduces wastage of food and at the same tie covers a larger section of students.
