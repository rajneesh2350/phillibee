Support & Services

“Phillibee Infrastructural support services” includes providing office along with office utilities, lounge, and reception with competent personnel to handle messages, secretarial services, Internet and telecom facilities, pantry and security, transaction processing, routine administration or accountancy, customer relationship management and tele marketing.

Following type of services connected with our business :

  • Evaluation of the Prospective Customers
  • Telemarketing
  • Process of purchase orders and their fulfillment services
  • Customer relationship Management service
  • Accounting and processing of transactions
  • Information and tracking of deliveries Schedule
  • Managing distribution and logistics
  • Operational assistance for marketing
  • Formulation of customer service and pricing policies
  • Infrastructural support services and other transaction processing
